# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Acme Corp! We are thrilled to have you join our team. This handbook contains important information about our company policies, benefits, and expectations.

## 2. Work Hours and Location
### 2.1 Standard Working Hours
Our standard office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM EST. Core working hours for all remote and in-office employees are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM EST. During core hours, employees are expected to be available for meetings and collaborative work.

### 2.2 Remote Work Policy
Acme Corp operates on a hybrid model. Employees are allowed to work remotely up to three days a week. Mondays and Thursdays are mandatory in-office days for local employees.

## 3. Compensation and Benefits
### 3.1 Payroll
Employees are paid on a bi-weekly basis, every other Friday. Direct deposit is mandatory for all employees.

### 3.2 Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off per year during their first three years of employment. After three years, this increases to 20 days. PTO does not roll over to the next calendar year; "use it or lose it" applies.

### 3.3 Sick Leave
Employees receive 8 days of paid sick leave annually. Unused sick leave can be carried over, up to a maximum of 20 days.

### 3.4 Health Insurance
Health, dental, and vision insurance options are available for all full-time employees starting on their 30th day of employment. The company covers 80% of the employee premium.

## 4. Code of Conduct
### 4.1 Dress Code
Acme Corp maintains a "business casual" dress code. However, on Fridays, we observe "casual Friday" where jeans and t-shirts are acceptable, provided they do not display offensive material.

### 4.2 Use of Company Equipment
Laptops, phones, and other equipment provided by the company remain the property of Acme Corp. They must be used primarily for business purposes. Modifying system settings without IT approval is strictly prohibited.

## 5. Security and Confidentiality
All employees must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA). Discussing upcoming product features, client data, or internal financial metrics outside the company is grounds for immediate termination.

## 6. IT Support
For any technical issues, please submit a ticket via the internal IT portal or email support@acmecorp.internal. Do not attempt to fix hardware issues yourself.
